I have been exploring development for a while . But , given the awkward personality that I have , combined with the lack of good projects , I have decided to code my own website entirely from scratch. Programming everysingle piece on my own , as needed . This website will be a place where I will put my life , not necessarily for some one to notice , but just for myself .

A little context for who I am as a person . I have been shaped by the people that I have in my life. I see my parents as humans who put a lot of effort , to make decisions while being torn apart by conflicting choices and still trying to be "good parents" . I appreciate that effort . I think I have inherited their courage . The other person that has a lot of influence on me is my girlfriend . She is like a soldier , just marching through her problems , head-on , relentlessly. It's a little frightening for me , how does she just march through everything . I take little time to process , but she just moves ahead , which I find fascinating.

As a result of that , I am becoming a person conflicted between my choices, trying to meet my expectations from myself but still marching through my problems one at a time . I don't know , if this combination is good or bad , but it has helped become a better person than who I was.
