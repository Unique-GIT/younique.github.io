My Dilemma

There's another reason I call myself a wanderer . It is because , I feel , that I'm kinder than the people around me and am kind of a "carrying my heart on my sleeve" kind of a guy . I don't hide and am blunt . Now , these personality traits in a human being , ladies and gentleman , is what is referred to as the traits of a "naive" person .

A "naive" person , who makes you feel good , who is a nice company to have , a person whose smile seems to be adorable and is also someone who you wish well for . But , that same person , is "too optimistic" , " is blind to the ways of the world " , is seen as someone not so ambitious , someone cute but not sexy , as someone you want to be around you , but not someone you want to be like. ( Saying this , because I come from a competitive environment and I see people "consuming" others around me to get ahead , to literally get all the good habits/traits/things from the other person ) .

A "naive" person , does irrational things hoping for things to turn out good . With proper execution , a lot of times things do turn out well , but sometimes they don't .

My problems

Meaning ??

Well , people around me try to look meaning in the "things" they come across and I just don't ! I am more intrigued by the people I meet in doing / knowing those "things" and the stories they carry .

Special things ....

I am in awe of a "thing" , not because of the "thing" itself but because of the thought behind it , the knower , the doer that came up with that "thing". The sheer audacity of that person to make that "thing" special is the real fascination .

It's like saying , " It's only when you lose someone , you realise , they were just an ordinary person , it's your thought that made them special ! ".

Just as in chess, you get all excited about an idea that's pretty mundane and doesn't even carry any meaning for somebody who doesn't follow chess , but it's the meaning the chess players , chess followers and chess fans give , it's the heart that you pour out that makes the idea special.

Also , what meaning ??

Now , that its established that I am "naive" , I will also clarify that I don't necessarily need any meaning / purpose to do something .

Just as Immanuel Kant said , I like to treat people and most things , as "ends" themselves rather than them being a "means" to something else . In this world , where everybody is working towards living an optimised life ( another romantic way of saying , a selfish life ) , you only do those things that give you something , meet those people that give something back to you .

And , apparently , this is also a "problem" for me , and I have to find my way around this .
